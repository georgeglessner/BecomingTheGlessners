FAQ

Where are the ceremony and reception being held?

Both the ceremony and reception will be held at the Spring Lake Yacht Club.



Will the ceremony and reception be indoors or outdoors?

Weather permitting, both the ceremony and reception will be outdoors. The ceremony will be on grass, so please wear appropriate footwear.



Is there a dress code?

Dress code is semi-formal attire. This includes a cocktail dress, jumpsuit, long flowy gown, and suit and tie.



Can I bring my kids? Do I have a plus one?

Due to space limitations, your formal invitation and RSVP card will indicate who is invited. Thank you so much for understanding!
