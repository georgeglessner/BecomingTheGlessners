George moved to Grand Haven, MI at the start of his sophomore year of highschool. Not long after moving would he meet Martha. He always had a thing for Martha, but wouldn't act on it until their senior year. They started dating in April of 2014. After highschool, George went to Grand Valley State University and Martha went to Michigan State University. Even though they went to different colleges, George would visit Martha almost every weekend. After college, Martha moved to Scottsdale, AZ to work for Honeywell, while George finished up his last semester of college. After George graduated, he moved out to Scottsdale to live with Martha. They spent 2 years in Arizona before moving back to call North Muskegon, MI their home.
